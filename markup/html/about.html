Meet the Founder

While growing up, Mr. Kiley Summers experienced some financial hardships and vowed to be a better steward of his financial obligations. He had a reality check in 2008; one year after he graduated from college, the consumer debt started piling up. Kiley was in $70K+ debt of which $30K was student loans. At that moment Kiley was reminded about his humbled beginnings watching his single mother barely make ends meet. From there Kiley began to follow “Dave Ramsey’s Debt Snowball Method” and manually began applying every little extra money he had to the smallest debt at the end of the month while paying the minimum on the others debts. Once he and his wife got married, their consumer debt grew over $100K+. However, in 2015 they became debt free! God then planted a vision with them to automate the process they used by leveraging everyday transactions; hence SpenDebt was birthed. SpenDebt is designed to assist people to pay off debt leveraging micropayments through everyday transactions!
